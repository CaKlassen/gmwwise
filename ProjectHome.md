A plugin for game maker to use the professional sound engine Audiokinetic Wwise (WaveWorks Interactive Sound Engine)

**supported version of Wwise :** 2012.1.2.

**supported plugins:**
  * Sine
  * Tone Generator
  * Silence
  * Audio Input
  * Delay
  * Parametric EQ
  * Matrix Reverb
  * Compressor
  * Expander
  * Peak Limiter
  * Roomverb
  * Pitch Shifter
  * Meter
